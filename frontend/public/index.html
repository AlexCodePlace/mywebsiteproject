Air Pollution Application

Enter a City, State/Region and Country. Example: Waterville, NH, US.

Air pollution data is provided by OpenWeather APIs.
Besides basic Air Quality Index, you can see data about polluting gases:
Nitrogen dioxide (NO2), Ozone (O3) and particulates (PM2.5 and PM10).

Here you can check results for you city relative to five CAQI ranges:
